# [Game Theory #2: Why Schools Suck](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kS-muAuq62E)

## Purposes

* Literacy as reading and writing.
* Core competencies. Critical thinking, cooperation, communication.
* Lifelong learning.

Most schools however are bad at achieving these purposes. One can argue they have the opposite effect, making students hate learning.

Literacy is decreasing, universities require to read paragraphs and watch videos, not read books.

Attension span decreases.

School does not teach collaboration because it's a competition between classmates.

School actually teaches to hate itself and therefore hate learning.
