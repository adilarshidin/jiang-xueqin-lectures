## Breakdown

As nations rise and fall the game they play changes.

In the beginning they need to come together and build a place where you can survive. Cooperation and religion give energy and dynamics. Priests and shamans as elite.

Over time the elite becomes hereditary so the bureaucracy is established and the game changes to obey it.

Elite overproduction happens.

People emigrate from there and warring states happen. Which is maximum openness and energy. The best people and inventions appear during this time.

Intermarriage between city-states creates equilibrium and war becomes organized to maintain it. Court politics start as struggle in-between the elites.

The faction that wins the court politics have these:

1) Secrecy
2) Trust
3) Coordination

All this is done through secret societies. Secrecy is maintained through hierarchy and compartmentalization, only the top know what is going. Trust is maintained through group transgression (ritual sacrifice basic type). Coordination is maintained through mythology (eschatology).

When empire emerges the game becomes the struggle between the secret societies. They become insular and corrupt, the state itself is not considered and divided. That is why the new societies could conquer them. Often they invite these new societies and absorb them into their secret societies so that they could help them. They now become wealthier through trade with them, more brazen through banditry and advanced through mercenary work. Eventually these people take over.
