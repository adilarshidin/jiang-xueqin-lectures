# [Game Theory #10: The Law of Asymmetry](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=t5oisJiorsU)

## Empire

1. Mass. Infinite amount of population from metropolis & allies / vassals.
2. Organization, science, technology and most advanced weaponry.
3. Depth. You can afford to lose a lot of wars and come back to fight with more resources.

Theoretically with those advantages empires should be eternal.

All the listed points are disadvantages in the long term.

* Mass leads to inequality, debt, slavery. People become complacent, lazy, unmotivated. They don't believe the government.
* Organization births elite, over time the elite becomes parasitic (rent-seeking behaviour). Elite overproduction.
* Depth leads to hubris, inability to admit mistakes.

## Underdog

1. Energy. Motivation to do things.
2. Openness. To ideas and people.
3. Cohesion. Unity and common purpose.

This defeats the empire.

## War

### USA

* Technology makes lazy and dependent on itself.
* Propaganda denies openness and debate.
* Wealth motivates allies, vassals and proxies to hustle you.

Problems:

1) Lack of political will.
2) Logistics and manufacturing. Everything is shipped to China.
3) Casualties. Can not afford to lose anyone.

### Iran

* Faith.
* Terrain.
* Nationalism. Persian identity, 5000 of civilization.

Problems:

1) Zealotry, blindness.
2) Terrain is a fortress but can be a prison if the supplies are disrupted.
3) Ethnically diverse country, lots of minorities.
