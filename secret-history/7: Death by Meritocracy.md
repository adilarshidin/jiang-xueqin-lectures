Over time less religious and Ivy League became social clubs, to build cohesion. Then the recognition came that population education is required, state schools (A&M agricultural & mechanical), the schools were build to train tradesmen. Then science and technology was needed, so copied german universities and such were research universities (university of Chicago, John Hopkins).

By 1900 there is this system. If you were a poor person you go to a tradeschool, get a job and make good money. If you wanted to be a professor you went to research universities. If you were rich you went to the social clubs which are Ivy League.

However now the social clubs are unhappy about this system because they become less relevant and research people would overtake the Ivy League.

So Harvard instituted scholarship program (SAT) to capture the best students in Ivy League. Now the rich alumni are unhappy, because now you has to study and are in class with smarter people than you.

New system is created where grades are not the only factor. A holistic system, so now you need to be a virtuous person.

All these changes are to ensure that Harvard stays the institution of power in USA.
