## Mycenaean Greece

During collapse of bronze age greeks now had polises. Polises were at constant war with each other. Centralization removed and innovation restored.

Because every citizen had to fight, every citizen had the right to speech. Before every decision debate was made. No censorship could be possible.

Every citizen had to educate themselves in rhetorics to be able to debate. Writing system changed from linear b to alphabet.

## Homer

### Trojan war

1. Zeus.
2. Hera, Athena, Aphrodite.

One day they discover a golden apple on Olympus which says "to the most beautiful". Which is why they fight and Zeus made a contest to judge them. He assigns Paris, prince of Troy, to judge. Each goddess try to bribe him. Hera offers a kingdom. Athena offers all the wisdom in the world. Aphrodite offers the best woman in the world. He picks the ultimate and marries Helen. Now Hera and Athena want revenge, they decide to destroy Paris and Troy as a whole. At this time however Helen is married to Menelao king of Sparta, his brother Agamemnon king of Argos. Agamemnon is also a king of Mycenaean Greece. They organize the army to retrieve Helen. 10 years battle. The most famous heroes of this time are Achilles the bravest and Odysseus the wisest.

### Iliad

Agememnon and the army arrived to Troy. However it's a walled city and greeks are pirates so they can not destroy it. They raid allies of Troy. Capture young girls as sex slaves. If the girl is of powerful family she can be ransomed and you have to return her to the family. Agamemnon does not want to return priest's daughter. Priest prays to Apollo who unleashes a plague and people are dying. Achilles confronts Agamemnon and convinces him to return the woman but on the condition that Achilles gives up his slave girl. He does not want to but Agamemnon insists as a king. Achilles responds saying if that is so he will never fight to the king again. After the meeting Achilles goes to Thetis a river goddess to ask her to ask Zeus to help trojans so that greeks beg Achilles to save them because I came to be a hero. Hector prince of Troy, brother of Paris, discovers that Achilles left and launches a strike which devastates greeks and he almost burns their ships which would lead to destruction of supply lines. Odysseus and other heroes beg Agamemnon to get Achilles back. The king offers his daughter and all the treasure if Achilles returns. Odysseus and heroes go to Achilles to convince him but he refuses because Agamemnon is not with them. So the king does not want to lose face and Achilles wants an apology. Eventually Achilles becomes nervous watching greeks lose and sends Patroclus his lieutenant to offer help, but greeks respond that they know Achilles has bad temper and will not help but maybe Patroclus can. So he forces a duel with Hector who kills Patroclus. Now Achilles and Agamemnon are good again since they both want Hector gone. Achilles kills him and proved to be the greatest warrior. He attaches his body to a chariot and rides it around the city and everyone including gods are disgusted. Gods now want to make peace between Achilles and Priam, father of Hector because it is important to bury the dead so that he finds peace. The gods send Priam to Achilles's tent, he is busy and Priam sits next to him. At this point Priam can easily stab him with a dagger and avenge his son. However he kneels before him and kisses his hand which stuns Achilles and makes him recognize that the father has more courage than him and he submits to him. The two forgive each other and weep together.

The real battle is in the human heart. Achilles was depressed because he knew he was responsible for the death of Patroclus, could not forgive himself. Priam by forgiving Achilles allowed him to forgive himself.

The story resolves the hardest problem of society: how do you forgive those that did wrong to you and how do you forgive yourself for doing wrong to others? Resolving this stops destruction and allows vitality.
