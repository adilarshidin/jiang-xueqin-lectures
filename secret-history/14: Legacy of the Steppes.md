## Conquest of civilization

From Mesopotamia people moved to Europe. There they could maintain agricultural practices because the weather was good. And the rivers are not as grand therefore no big cities can appear. The property is communal, there is no war or conflict.

They also moved to steppes, which are vast grasslands, so you can not grow food there but you can keep livestock. Livestock is a valuable property, which can be stolen, thus conflict arises. Milk and meat make you tall and strong. To move around the steppes horse-riding is accomplished. To move your belongings wheel and wagon are invented.

Steppes is a training ground. So when nomads encounter civilizations they conquer them.
