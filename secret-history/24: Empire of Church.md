dynasty in China waged war at this time against steppes forcing them to migrate, which created a chain migration into Europe. The goths were immigrants in the empire and had higher birthrate than romans, so the empire decided to incorporate them into the army as mercenaries. The catholic church was advantageus in that it could convert the immigrants as well as integrate their leaders, war chiefs, into the nobility.

## Constantine

If the empire is to have an official religion there needs to be orthodoxy to avoid division.

At this time 2 theological points are debated:

1. Christology. Nature of Christ, divine or human. who is Mary. The church resolved it by Hipostatic Union (both fully human and fully divine). Nestorians objected and were persecuted, so had to go to the middle east. There they taught that Jesus was a human, not a God, just a "messenger".
2. Nature of Trinity and relationships between them. Modalism - different modes that change at any given moment. Partialism - The Trinity are all a part of the same divine force. Arianism - God came first and afterwords created Jesus.

Council of Nicaea to resolve these problems. They established The Holy Trinity. God, Jesus and The Holy Spirit are separate identities but are equal.

The Byzantine empire will enforce the results of the council which will start series of conflicts.
