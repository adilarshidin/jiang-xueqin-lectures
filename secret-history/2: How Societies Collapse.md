## Reasons of decline

### Financialization

Thomas Piketty "Capital in the 21st century".

Capitalism transitions from face to face. In the beginning consumer capitalism: creating goods that consumers want to buy. Focus on generation of wealth. Then financial capitalism: investment into stock market. Focus on generation of money. Monopoly capitalism: a few companies control everything.

The further it goes the less people want to work and more want to speculate.

Enterprenuer starts a business. Creates wealth because creates products and hires people. Then with made money invests to make more money. Now he creates money and not wealth because no one is working for that money increase. The problem is that stock market advances but the manufacturing stays the same.

The real economy 2%, financial 5%. So if you put 1kk in a business you make at most 20k, but if put in stocks you make 50k.

### Elite overproduction

Peter Turchin.

Too many wealthy powerful people compete for limited positions of power.

Rat utopia. John Calhoun. A room sealed off from external world. The room has food, water, housing for rats. They multiplied quickly and ultimately killed each other no matter how the experiment was configured. In the world of abundance and security the rats kill each other.

The theory is that rats did not compete for food or territory but for status. In the wild rats establish an alpha male and if not run away somewhere else to find a female. But in the experiment there is nowhere to run.

Status is a zero sum game.

Children of the elite always fight for the position of power. Eventually they have too much children and no enough position of power. Eventually leads to war or revolution.

### Civilizational lifecycle

Oswald Spengler.

Society has a lifecycle the same as a human. Eventually it dies. Village -> town -> city -> megapolis.

The village life is simple. People work hard, are united and have collective mentality. A lot of children.

With progression of society abstraction increases. In the village you understand perfectly what you eat and drink and where it comes from. In the city you have no idea. So you are more removed from reality.

In the village relationships hold people together. In the city - money. That means we don't have to trust each other. In the village if you get sick your family or neighbors come to help. In the city you go to a doctor and pay. So you don't want to work now, you want immigrants to work for you; you don't care about other people but yourself; you don't want to have children.

External threat will not healp because you are too selfish to be aware of a threat, also you can not collaborate with each other.

## Coherent theory

At the core of the civilization stand a small group of families.

These families exact power and control all aspects of society through finance (today central banking), religion (today science), intelligence.

The people are on the outer edge and generate wealth. System is designed to generate as much energy as possible from the people.

When society rises the families are happy to let the managers (systems in place) to control society, they provide feedback to families and the people are treated well.

But because there are eventually too many elite children which start the conflict within. Now the system has to exploit people to keep its standard of living.

Elites split into factions which take under their wing parts of the system and groups of people. Now revolution or civil war starts. Or a faction can invite aliens to try and destroy other factions and take a bigger part of society, this is how a war starts.
